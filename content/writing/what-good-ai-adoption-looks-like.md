Most AI initiatives begin with a model and then search for a use case. The stronger sequence is the opposite.

Start with workflow mapping. Where are people spending time? Which decisions are high-frequency and low-risk? Where does quality degrade because context is fragmented?

Then decide what role AI should play: drafting, classification, synthesis, quality checks, or recommendation.

Good adoption is usually quiet. It appears as fewer handoff errors, faster onboarding, and better internal documentation. It does not always look like a product launch.

The future of AI at work is not just technical capability. It is institutional capability.
